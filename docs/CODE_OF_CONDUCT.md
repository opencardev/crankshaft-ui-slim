# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, safe, and productive environment for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience level, or personal characteristics.

## Expected Behavior

All contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional in communication
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating
- Give and accept constructive feedback
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks
- Help maintain an inclusive and collaborative community

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- Personal attacks, insults, or threats
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive, hostile, or intentionally obstructive conduct

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Repository discussions, pull requests, and issues
- Project communication channels
- Any official project collaboration space

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to project maintainers.
Include relevant context, screenshots/logs, and links when possible.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate actions in response to violations, including:

- Warning the contributor
- Removing or editing offending content
- Temporary or permanent ban from project spaces

Enforcement decisions are made to protect the health and safety of the community.
